Secure Space . What is it?

Securespace is a secure communications software developed to facilitate a confidential online counselling and psychotherapy service. The software in its generic form can also be used to provide any online professional services that require similar fully secure communication, and where the identity of both parties is verified.

As more and more services transition to the Internet - Gmail and Google docs, online banking and other services, security is becoming increasingly important. This is vital for protecting sensitive information that may be exchanged as part of the counselling process. Though many services are transferring to online format there is not enough attention on security. Secure space is a software product that has been developed for the healthcare and other markets where secure communications are required for professional practice.

What features does Securespace offer?

  • Addresses problem of not being able to verify that the user is who they say they are. Even in the case where somebody might access a person’s username and password they will not be able to access the person’s data - in this case counselling correspondences between client and counsellor. Client side certificates ensure this.
  • Passwords are subject to one-way encryption so that even someone with full access to the system cannot get your password.
  • Digital certificates are generated for clients on a separate off-line secure server.
  • Fully secure gateway into the system
  • Offers an equivalent security model to that used by banks and other high security institutions; where large sums of money and sensitive personal information are being transacted.
  • A very simple intuitive application to use with full usability testing in place
  • Integration of assessment forms and intake procedures for clinical counselling
  • All communications are encrypted using secure socket layer technology.
  • Ability to incorporate evaluative instruments such as session ratings and outcome data collection
  • There is encryption of messages exchanged between users of the system down to database level.
  • The program integrate a case management system for administration of clients and workloads
